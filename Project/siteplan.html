Website Project Plan

Site Name

New Delhi Travel Guide

This name was selected because the website will help users explore the culture, attractions, food, and history of New Delhi. It is simple and clearly tells visitors what the site is about.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide travelers with helpful information about New Delhi. The site will include important tourist spots, travel tips, food recommendations, local transportation, and a short cultural overview. It will serve as a simple guide for anyone planning to visit the city.

Scenarios

  • “What are the best places to visit in New Delhi for a first-time traveler?”
  • “Where can I find good local food and safe transportation options in the city?”

Color Scheme

I selected two main colors for this website. These colors match India’s culture and give a warm, inviting feeling.

Primary Color – Dark Green (#004d40)
Used for headings, navigation bar, and accents.

Accent Color – Warm Orange (#ff7043)
Used for buttons, highlights, and section headers.

Typography

Heading Font: Poppins (Google Font) – clean and modern, good for titles.

Body Font: Arial – simple to read and works well on all devices.
